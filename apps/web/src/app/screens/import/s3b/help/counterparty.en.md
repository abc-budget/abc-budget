**Counterparty** is the other side of a financial transaction — the payee, the sender of a transfer, the
merchant, and so on. Unlike the «**Description**» column (which explains the *purpose* of a transaction),
the counterparty identifies **whom** the transaction was with.
Some banks provide the counterparty in a separate column. If that's your case, you can mark it as
«**Counterparty**» and use it in your «_rules_» for categorization — **separately** from the description.
This lets you write more precise rules (e.g. by a specific payee, not just by the purpose text).
This data type needs no additional configuration.
